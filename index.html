“Reggi Roomers is a Detroit-based artist creating unique sounds in the bedroom pop genre. Drawing inspiration from indie pop, experimental lo-fi, and soulful elements, Reggi's music is an eclectic mix of catchy hooks and introspective lyrics. With a melancholic twist, their music explores the highs and lows of life through an experimental lens. Although shrouded in mystery, Reggi's authentic sound and emotional depth are sure to leave a lasting impression on listeners.”
